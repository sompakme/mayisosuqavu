Early Life, Biography & Education

Hyacinth is part of a Christian family. He holds a mixed ethnicity. According to Wikipedia, Hyacinth took birth on Saturday, May 14, 1966, in Mbangur, Mbadede, Vandeikya, Benue State, Nigeria. Talking about his age then, Hyacinth is 56 years old (as of 2022). His birthday falls on the 14th of every May. Hyacinth is also known as Rev. Fr. Hyacinth Iormem Alia. After seeing his Wikipedia profile, we found that Hyacinth is a well-educated person.

He got his First School Leaving Certificate (FSLC) at St. Francis Primary School in Agidi, Mbatiav, in the Gboko Local Government Area of Benue State. After that, he went to St. James Minor Seminary in Makurdi, where he got his General Certificate of Education (GCE) in 1983.

Later, Alia was taken admission to St. Augustine’s Major Seminary in Jos, where he received a Diploma in Religious Studies in 1987 and a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Sacred Theology in 1990 from the Pontifical Urban University, Rome, in accordance with his desire and commitment to fulfill the Lord’s call as a Catholic Priest.

In 1999, Alia earned a Master of Arts degree in Religious Education (Psychology and Counseling) at Fordham University in New York City. He completed his second Master of Arts degree in Biomedical Ethics in 2004 at Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. In 2005, he went on to earn a Doctorate in Biomedical Ethics from the same university. After that, he began focusing on his career.
