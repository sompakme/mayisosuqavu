WWE World Heavyweight Title Match Set For 1/15 RAW

WWE RAW will emanate from Little Rock, Arkansas next week, and there are a lot of plans. The WWE World Heavyweight Championship is on the line, but that’s not all.

Jinder Mahal got mouthy with Seth Rollins during WWE RAW this week. Rollins is a fighting champion, so he won’t waste any time in defending that title.

Next week’s WWE RAW will see Seth Rollins defend the WWE World Heavyweight Championship against Jinder Mahal. No special stipulation was set for this match, but it will be for the tile.

The WWE RAW card for January 15 includes matchups such as The Miz & R-Truth vs Finn Balor & Damian Priest and #DIY vs DJ McDonagh & Dominik Mysterio. Additionally, GUNTHER makes his return, adding an element of anticipation to the upcoming episode.

We will have more on WWE’s plan for RAW next week, right here at Ringside News. You never know what will break next, but we will be here with all the headlines you need.

WWE RAW Card for January 15th:

  • Seth Rollins vs Jinder Mahal – WWE World Heavyweight Championship
  • The Miz & R-Truth vs Finn Balor & Damian Priest
  • #DIY vs DJ McDonagh & Dominik Mysterio
  • GUNTHER Returns
