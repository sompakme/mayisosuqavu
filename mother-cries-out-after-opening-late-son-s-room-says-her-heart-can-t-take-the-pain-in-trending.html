Mother Cries Out After Opening Late Sons Room, Says Her Heart Cant Take the Pain, in Trending

  • A heartbroken mother has shared a sad video on TikTok showing the moment she opened her late son's room
  • In the video, she revealed that she thought she was finally strong enough to go into the room after his death
  • Sadly, after opening the room, she ran out of the place immediately because her heart couldn't bear the agony

A grieving mother could not hide her pain after opening the door of her late son's room.

In a video, the sad woman revealed that she planned to enter his room days after his demise, but her heart failed her.

According to her, as soon as she opened the door, she began to shake and feel so much pain. This made her shut the door immediately and leave the room.
