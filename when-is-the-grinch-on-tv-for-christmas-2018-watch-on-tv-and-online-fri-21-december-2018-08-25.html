The Grinch is one of the most popular Christmas movies in history.

Since its release in 2000, How the Grinch Stole Christmas has become a cult classic and a must-watch film to get you in the mood for the festive period.

Although there is a cartoon remake that recently hit U.K cinemas, nothing could ever compete with Jim Carrey’s classic.

When is the Grinch on TV 2018?

The next Freeview showing off The Grinch will be on Sunday, December 23rd.

It will air at 7 pm on ITV2, straight after Elf!

Finally, The Grinch (2000) will also air on Christmas Eve! Simply sit down in front of ITV at 12:55 pm to watch it.

Is The Grinch on Netflix?

No!

The Grinch (2000) is not available to watch on U.K Netflix right now.

Is The Grinch on Sky?

No!

The Grinch (2000) is not part of the Sky Cinema Christmas Collection. However, it is rumoured to be joining their Christmas roster closer to the big day.

For now, your safest bet is to watch or record The Grinch it on ITV2.
