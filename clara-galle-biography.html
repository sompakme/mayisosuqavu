Clara Galle Biography

QUICK FACTS
Nationality Spain
Birth April 15, 2002 (Pamplona)

BIOGRAPHY
Since she was little, Clara Galle showed great interest in acting, which is why she studied performing arts at the Plaza de la Cruz Institute. Since 2019, Galle began her career as the face of numerous important brands and collaborating with brands such as Nyx, Arizona Vintage and Kaotiko, among others. In 2020 she moved to Madrid to study Audiovisual Design at the Complutense University of Madrid. In October 2021 she starred in the video clip for Sebastián Yatra’s single “Tacones rojo”. She was recently confirmed to be part of the cast of the second season of the Amazon Prime Video series El Internado: Las Cumbres (2022), where she plays Eva. We will soon see her in the leading role of Raquel Mendoza in the Netflix original film Through My Window (2022), based on the novel of the same name by Ariana Godoy, this being her first experience on the big screen.
