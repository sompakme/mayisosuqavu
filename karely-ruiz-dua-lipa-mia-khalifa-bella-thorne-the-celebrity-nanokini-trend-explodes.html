Karely Ruiz, Dua Lipa, Mia Khalifa, Bella Thorne: The celebrity nanokini trend explodes

Microbikinis are in fashion among celebrities and more and more of them are taking advantage of the summer sun to show their bodies in them.

Influencers and celebrities are choosing increasingly tiny bikini models that defy censorship on social networks, which is why nanokinis are characterized by being pieces that barely cover their intimate parts.

English singer Dua Lipa launched this new swimming costume fashion, but influencers around the world have followed suit, making it the hit item of 2023.

In Mexico alone there are numerous personalities who have joined the new wave of the nanokinis from Belinda to Sofia Rivera Torres, Karely Ruiz, Mia Khalifa...

Dua Lipa

British singer Dua Lipa is one of the main figures of this trend. She recently posed on her social networks with a Hello Kitty two-piece bikini and another one by Patricio Estrella.

Both models fascinated so many women that even Bella Thorne and Mia Khalifa opted to get these trendy bikinis.

Bella Thorne

Thorne rose to fame at the age of 13 as the star of the Disney Channel series "Shake It Up" with Zendaya.

In fact, Thorne relaunched her career on the adult platform OnlyFans, taking her to 50,000 subscribers in one week.

Two years later, the actress surpassed 25,000,000 followers (August 2022).

Mia Khalifa

Mia Khalifa is a retired pornographic actress, model and internet celebrity born in Beirut, capital of Lebanon, on 10 February 1993.

A native of the United States, she became known for the popularity of her adult videos.

This one was no exception and the influencer uploaded a provocative photo showing a nanokini that features Patrick Star from the SpongeBob series on her breasts and a matching pastel pink mini bottom.

Karely Ruiz

Karely Ruiz, originally from Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, has become one of the most popular influencers in the country with millions of followers on Instagram.

On many occasions she chooses to wear this type of microbikini, as on this occasion she opted for pieces of synthetic fabric from SpongeBob.
