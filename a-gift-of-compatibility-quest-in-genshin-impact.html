A Gift of Compatibility quest in Genshin Impact

There is a new quest, Garcia’s Paean: A Gift of Compatibility, that some Genshin Impact players can unlock by completing a specific Commission. However, it is a Sumeru Commission. Players can only get Commissions in Sumeru if they complete The Morn a Thousand Roses Brings Archon Quest.

The actual Commission required for this quest is part of the Garcia's Paean series. Completing them should be quite easy for every player, but the luck in acquiring them makes unlocking Garcia's Paean: A Gift of Compatibility a tad difficult.

Requirements to unlock Garcia's Paean: A Gift of Compatibility quest in Genshin Impact

To unlock Garcia’s Paean: A Gift of Compatibility, Genshin Impact players must have completed:

  • Garcia’s Paean
  • Garcia’s Paean: Key Items
  • Garcia’s Paean: Substitute

Players must remember that the above quests are RNG based, and there is no guaranteed way to get these commissions as per their preference.

Garcia’s Paean

There is a World Quest called Garcia’s Paean in Liyue that’s required to be completed in order to do these Commissions. It’s located at the Wangshu Inn, but Travelers should know that the Archon Quest Wangshu uses one of the important NPCs for this quest.

Thus, they should complete Wangshu before attempting Garcia’s Paean. This quest was released in Genshin Impact 2.7, so there’s a chance that the player has already done it.

Garcia’s Paean: Key Items

The above video shows off one Commission that is required to unlock Garcia’s Paean: A Gift of Compatibility. Basically, Travelers have to do the following:

  • Have a lengthy conversation with Garcia.
  • Defeat some nearby slimes.
  • Return to Garcia.

    • It’s something every Genshin Impact player should be capable of doing. However, there is no way to guarantee that Travelers will obtain this specific Commission. After all, they can get a fair selection of other Sumeru Commissions instead of this one.

    Remember, players can only get four Commissions per day.

    One can open up their Adventurer Handbook and change the "Choose preferred region in which to do Commission Quests" from the current setting to Sumeru. Doing so will greatly increase the odds of getting Garcia’s Paean: Key Items.

    Garcia’s Paean: Substitute

    The above video explains how to complete this quest. For a better understanding, here is the basic premise:

  • Talk to Garcia.
  • Give him either two Treasure Hoarder Insignias, two Silver Raven Insignias, or two Golden Raven Insignias.

    • As with the previous Commission, there is no guarantee that players will get this one. These items are easy to obtain, so once they complete this Commission, it’s time to do the main quest readers want to unlock.

    The World Quest doesn’t require any specific Daily Resets like the Commissions do, meaning one can begin with it immediately.

    Garcia’s Paean: A Gift of Compatibility

    After completing all the prerequisite quests, players should be able to unlock “Garcia’s Paean: A Gift of Compatibility” quest. However, the new quest just won’t show up on the quest menu as the final quest is yet to be playable on the current version of Genshin Impact (V3.2).

    Once Garcia’s Paean: A Gift of Compatibility is done, Travelers can get the Sumeru Reputation EXP they rightfully deserve.
