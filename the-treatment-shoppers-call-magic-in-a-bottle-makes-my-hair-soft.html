The Treatment Shoppers Call Magic in a Bottle Makes My Hair Soft

I’m at my most vulnerable sitting in the salon chair — it’s that unflattering black cape paired with the nerves of a new look — so if you tell me my hair needs a certain product, I’ll probably grab it on my way out. Of course, this is how I’ve found myself with a bathroom cabinet full of styling sprays and gels I rarely pick up, but there’s one product that was recommended to me years ago that I used to its last drop, and have since repurchased again and again.

Davines’ Oi All in One Milk is my haircare holy grail; I’ve moved cities and changed salons, but this one product has remained a constant in my many stylists’ routines as well as mine. With just a few spritzes, my strands become silky, soft, manageable, and the best they’ve ever smelled. And right now, I’m restocking at Amazon where the leave-in conditioner is $40.

Davines’ Oi All in One Milk Treatment

I’ve tried various multi-step hair care routines that involve conditioners, glosses, and oils, but ultimately, I keep coming back to this one-and-done leave-in treatment. The spray is designed to detangle, hydrate strands, reduce frizz, and act as a heat protectant. And while I love that I don’t need to layer yet another product on top if I want to quickly hit it with a blowdryer, what I adore even more is how soft and shiny it leaves my dry, frizzy hair.

For years leading up to trying the Oi All in One Milk, it was anyone’s guess how my hair might look post-shower. But once I introduced the treatment to my routine, almost everyday became a hair-down day. It tackled my dryness, leaving my strands silky and shiny, and softened my hair for more defined waves. Tangles went away almost entirely, and the brittleness caused by multiple bleach jobs slowly started to dissipate. Not to be dramatic, but I’d describe its effectiveness as near-miraculous; so it’s not shocking to me that the spray has amassed more than 10,300 fans at Amazon.

“I have double processed hair with highlights, so my hair is dry and weak,” explained one person before writing that the Oi milk gave them their “softest, healthiest hair.” Another customer whose hair “has been through a lot of coloring, bleaching, and heat styling” agreed, writing that it had “never really felt truly soft until” using the Davines treatment. “It’s a game-changer for damaged hair,” they raved. And a shopper with “kinky” gray hair even called it “magic in a bottle.” “My hair feels like healthy hair again and my grays are laying down,” they gushed, adding that it “keeps [their] hair feeling healthy and frizz free for days.”
