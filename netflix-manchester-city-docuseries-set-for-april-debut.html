Netflix Manchester City Docuseries Set for April Debut

Netflix is taking on the Premier League, picking up a docuseries chronicling one of the league’s top teams, Manchester City.

The streaming giant has picked up the six-part docuseries Together: Treble Winners, which follows the team during their championship 2022-2023 season. The series had cameras rolling when star Erling Haaland arrived with the team, and during its preseason in the U.S., all the way through the final weeks of the season, the FA Cup Final at Wembley Stadium and the club’s first Champions League Final victory in Istanbul.

Together: Treble Winners will debut globally on Netflix April 2, and was produced by the club’s in-house production team City Studios.

“Manchester City has an innovative and industry leading approach centred around our in- house creative and production hub, City Studios, and it is testament to the talent and hard work of the team that the latest documentary series will be released on Netflix,” said Nuria Tarre, chief marketing and dan experience officer at City Football Group, in a statement. “The club was the first to allow cameras behind the scenes back in 2018 with All or Nothing and over the last four years we’ve had an embedded crew following our team every season to create the best, exclusive content that isn’t usually shown in football. Today’s announcement is the next step in our journey as one of the leading global sports entertainment organisations and we hope our fans and everyone around the world enjoys.

“To win the Treble was a truly special moment for this Club and every single person involved from our players, our coaches, our backroom staff all worked so hard every single day to achieve what we did,” added Manchester City Manager Pep Guardiola. “I am so proud that we continue to fight for our success, for the next trophy, for the next game. We will always remember this incredible time and I hope our fans do when watching this new series.”

Netflix has become arguably the biggest buyer of sports docs, with series following leagues, teams and players in the NFL, MLB, NBA, NASCAR, Formula 1, pro tennis and other sports. The Premier League, however, has not been the focus of a docuseries until now (though one of Netflix’s biggest sports docs last year chronicled the life of legendary Manchester United star and Miami F.C. owner David Beckham).
