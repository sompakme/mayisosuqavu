How can I get free shipping at True Religion?

You can enjoy free ground shipping on orders of over $150 at True Religion. If your order is less than this, Economy Shipping costs $9.99. Your order should be shipped within 8-10 working days with Economy Shipping. You can also check this page for the occasional True Religion discount code for free shipping on future orders. 

Does True Religion have a student discount?

Yes, at True Religion students can enjoy free shipping on all orders as well as up to 60% off sitewide. Simply verify your student status via Student Beans account to confirm your student ID. Once you’ve logged into your account, you should be able to see your True Religion coupon.

How can I get 15% off at True Religion?

Does True Religion accept returns?

The True Religion returns policy states that unwanted clothing can be returned up to 30 days after the date of purchase in exchange for a full refund or an exchange. This means that you're free to order clothing that you can return if it's not quite right.
