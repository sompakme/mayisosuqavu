Nirvair Pannu (Singer) Wiki, Biography, Age, Parents, Girlfriend, Height, Songs, Net Worth &amp; More

Nirvair Pannu (born in 1999; Age: 23 years old) is a renowned Punjabi singer, lyricist, media face, and social media star from Punjab, India. He is known in the country for his amazing vocal tone and Punjabi songs. He shared that he started singing during his childhood and continue his passion in college as well.

Who is Nirvair Pannu, Punjabi Singer?

This famous young singer released his first song after completing his senior secondary education. He got hit the region with his song ‘Bandook’, which was released in 2020. The song received more than 4 Million views within a few days of releasing the songs. Some of his other songs include Salute, City of Gold, Mitha Bolke, Pistol, and many more.

From his official Instagram account, we got to know that he has recently released a song titled ‘Martyrs’, which is based on the martyrdom of Guru Gobind Singh Ji and 4 Sahibzaade. It was released on 24 December 2022 on the official YouTube channel of ‘Juke Dock Devotional’.

Career- Singing, Punjabi Songs, and Albums

Nirvair Pannu shared during an interview that he used to sing during Nagar Kirtan in the village and received a lot of appreciation from the villagers for his melodious voice. Also, he sang during school and college. He was a member of his school and college Bhangra team and sang ‘Boliya’ during competitions. With a lot of practice, his voice became even more beautiful and amazing.

In 2018, his first song titled ‘Pistol’ got released. He mostly sings songs of the Indian Film Pop genre and has signed a contract with Juke Dock Record Label. In 2020, his song ‘Bandook’ got released and got viral within no time. The song has hundreds of millions of views on YouTube.

Afterward, he also released many other songs as well such as City of Gold, Call, Sher Sardar, Kurta Pajama, Weekend, Sawa Lakh Dilliye, Itihas, Don’t Know Why, Salute, Arhi Jatt Di, Turban King, many more. In August 2022, he released an RP titled ‘Click’ which has 5 songs.
