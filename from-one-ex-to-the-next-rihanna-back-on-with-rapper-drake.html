From One Ex To The Next: Rihanna Back on With Rapper Drake?

It seems that Rihanna just can't stay away from her exes.

After making it rain at a strip club in Texas together over a week ago, the pop star and her former lover Drake were once again seen partying together at a Los Angeles nightclub.

The pair were pictured arriving at Bootsy Bellows at around 1am on Tuesday morning after the rapper performed in concert at the Staples Center Downtown.

The Take Care singer closed down the club so he could host a private after party, which reportedly lasted until 4.30am morning.

The 27-year-old was clearly in a celebratory mood, as he arrived at the bash already with a drink in his hand.

Meanwhile, Rihanna made sure to look her very best for the night out with her ex, showing off plenty of skin in a pair of tiny denim Daisy Dukes and a white cropped top underneath a pal pink jacket.

Edit She elongated her slender legs even further by donning a pair of white strappy sandals on her feet.

The Diamonds singer, who scooped up two awards at the American Music Awards Awards on Sunday night, was still rocking her slicked back 'bed head' hairstyle. '

The Barbadian beauty's new 'do had already garnered a lot of buzz in the Twitterverse during the AMAs.

RiRi had smoothed her black bob down with numerous bobby pins - a fuss-free style known as a 'doobie wrap' typically worn at night to keep relaxed hair neat while sleeping.
