Diane Keaton Wore Only a Shirt and Over-the-Knee Boots

Diane Keaton is goals. The actress was spotted on Wednesday on the set of her new movie Mack and Rita, currently filming in Palm Springs, and it's no surprise she looked amazing. I mean, after all, she is a fashion icon. But this look strayed quite a bit from her signature style (hat, big belt, etc.)

The actress was photographed in sky-high, over-the-knee snakeskin printed boots (which look incredibly hard to walk in). She paired the boots with nothing but an oversized, white button down shirt. To complete the look, Keaton sported a long silver wig.

The movie will follow the journey of a 30-something-year-old woman Mackenzie who is sick of her 30s and trying to prevent aging. She aspires to be like her grandmother: sure of herself, comfortable in her own skin, and not afraid of saying "no." During Mackenzie's best friend's bachelorette party in Palm Springs, Mackenzie enters a sound bath regression pod and leaves as a 70-year-old woman, whom she names Rita (Keaton).

The movie will also star Elizabeth Lail (Mackenzie), Taylour Paige, Wendie Malick, Simon Rex, Dustin Milligan, and Wipeout host Nicole Byer.

Well, if my closet looks anything like Diane Keaton's at 70, I'll consider my life successful.
