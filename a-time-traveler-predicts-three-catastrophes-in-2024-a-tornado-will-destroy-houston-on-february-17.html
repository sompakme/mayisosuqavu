A 'time traveler' predicts three catastrophes in 2024: A tornado will destroy Houston on February 17

Eno Alaric, is a TikToker who accumulates more than 658,000 followers, and this one has the curiosity of claiming to be a 'time traveler' who comes from the year 2671. In fact, in one of his latest videos he has made a prediction of what will happen this coming 2024. He speaks of three monstrous catastrophes.

Now, against him is that he has never gotten a single one of his predictions right, but his followers continue to show interest.

For starters, he has made this introduction. "2024 is considered the worst year in history in terms of natural disasters."

The three 'cursed' dates of 2024

And so he has given way to his three predictions for the coming year. February 17, 2024 will see "the first F6 tornado in Houston, Texas (USA)," and Eno Alaric notes that this tornado will be "more than 17 miles wide and destroys almost the entire city".

The second date to note will be March 20, 2024: "A mile-high dust storm occurs in the Mid-Eastern United States. It extends from Missouri to North Carolina and lasts for months."

While the third prediction will be on April 2, 2024: "A magnitude 9.8 earthquake named Big John occurs on the San Andreas fault. This earthquake leaves San Francisco, Los Angeles and other cities in ruins Big John triggers a mega tsunami that hits the California coast".
