Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders: A Complete Relationship Timeline

December 18: Wonders and Davidson hit up the most romantic spot in New York…Whole Foods. A rep says they’re just friends. Sure, Jan.

December 20: Sources catch Wonders heading into Davidson’s Manhattan apartment.

December 27: Pete and EmRata are officially over. “Their fling has moved into the friend zone,” a source reveals, adding that the breakup is “fine with both of them.”

January 2023

January 9: Davidson and Wonders get papped in chilly Brooklyn looking awfully snuggly while waiting for takeout pierogis.

January 15: Seeing a lot of each other! They go out in New York. It’s unclear where.

January 19: They hold hands at Universal Studios. Aww. Of note: This is in California. They seem to be making their way west to…

January 23: …Hawaii, where photographers catch them getting playful in the water and smooching on the beach. Some savvy marketing person leaks the most important detail of all: Wonders’s green bikini is from Cami and Jax, per Page Six. And Davidson’s Kardashian-inspired tattoos appear to be gone. Unless…they were never real…to begin with? It’s been done before.

March 5: The couple is involved in a single-car crash in Beverly Hills, according to multiple reports.

People notes that Davidson was driving when his car collided with a fire hydrant before running into the side of a home. No one was injured in the crash, however Davidson was charged with one misdemeanor count of reckless driving.

Spring 2023: In interviews with the press, both Davidson and Wonders gush about their relationship.

“She’s the best. She’s the best actress,” Davidson said in an April interview with Entertainment Tonight at the premiere of his semi-autobiographical show, Bupkis. “She’s gonna crush. She’s got a lot of cool stuff going so.… I had a blast.”
