The Devils True Identity Narrowed Down as Options Diminish

MJF has been involved in a major storyline featuring an individual wearing his Devil mask that has been stalking and harassing him for the past two months. The identity of the Devil remains a big topic of discussion and now another name has been ruled out for The Devil’s identity.

Last week’s Dynamite saw MJF’s feud with the person posing as The Devil see some development. It mostly saw MJF being knocked unconscious backstage by what fans assume was The Devil.

This week’s Dynamite would then see Adam Page getting attacked by The Devil’s henchmen to close out the show. As previously reported, one big name isn’t involved in the storyline.

While speaking during Fightful Select’s Q&A session, Sean Ross Sapp was asked if Tony Khan would be revealed as The Devil. Sapp noted that he had not heard anything about Khan being The Devil and as far as he knows, Tony doesn’t want an on-screen character for his televised shows.

”Tony has long not wanted to be a regular on-screen character for AEW or at least as of a few years ago. I have not heard anything that indicate he is the Devil.”

It remains to be seen who will end up being revealed as the man behind The Devil’s Mask. With AEW Worlds end set to take place on December 30th, it is likely fans will finally get to know who The Devil will be at the show.
