Copenhagen Fashion Week AW24 - everything you need to see

Rotate closed this season's Copenhagen Fashion Week with a collection dedicated to 1950s elegance and glamour, while keeping its distinctive brand aesthetic. Shimmering sequins, classic black mini-dresses, XL rose corsages and floral embellishments all embody the brand's much-loved style and give the collection a nostalgic feel, while being designed to be also worn as separate pieces, as well as full looks.

"This provides the wearer with the opportunity to experiment with various personalities and customise their look according to their own preferences," designers Thora Valdimars and Jeanette Madsen explain." It's a nod to the versatility inherent in our designs and an invitation for individuals to make the collection uniquely their own."

For autumn/winter 2024, Rotate also presented eight new wedding dress designs, as a tribute to Elizabeth Taylor's eight marriages. The styles revisit Rotate's most iconic silhouettes in all-white hues; the designs are every modern bride's dream.
