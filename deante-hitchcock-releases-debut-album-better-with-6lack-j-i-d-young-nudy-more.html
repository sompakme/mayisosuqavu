Deante' Hitchcock Releases Debut Album &quot;Better&quot; With 6LACK, J.I.D, Young Nudy, &amp; More

One of the most underlooked talents from Atlanta, Deante' Hitchcock is about to open so many new eyes to his music with his debut studio album Better. The rapper has been itching for his golden ticket for years and finally, his time has come.

The introspective storyteller releases his brand new album Better today, which includes featured appearances from J.I.D, 6LACK, Young Nudy, Miguel, and more. Right off the bat, you'll understand why Deante' Hitchcock is one of the greater hidden gems of Atlanta. His musicality shines through and his lyricism will please even the most selective hip-hop fan.
