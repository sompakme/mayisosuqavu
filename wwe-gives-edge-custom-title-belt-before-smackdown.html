WWE Gives Edge Custom Title Belt Before SmackDown

WWE knows how to give someone flowers when they want to. Edge is celebrating 25 years of his career in Toronto tonight, and it is a very big deal.

In honor of his 25-year anniversary on SmackDown, Edge has been presented with a unique custom belt. During the August 18th episode of WWE SmackDown, Edge will mark his 25-year milestone since making his main roster debut by stepping into the ring against Sheamus in their first-ever encounter.

As WWE paid tribute to the fact that tonight’s match is the final match on his current WWE contract before it comes to an end, “The Rated R Superstar” has shared this significant moment with his fans. He also received a nice custom title belt from WWE in the process.

WWE took to social media to share a video capturing the moment Edge received the specially crafted belt in anticipation of his remarkable 25-year journey on SmackDown.

Tonight’s episode of SmackDown should be a big one. Edge has accomplished a lot during his time with WWE, and tonight will certainly be a monumental night for Edgeheads all over.
