What is Nate Burleson's net worth?

NATE Burleson is a former athlete turned host who joined the CBS Mornings show panel in 2021.

Here's all to know about Burleson's earnings from his reign as an NFL player to CBS Morning's talk show host.

What is Nate Burleson's net worth?

Born in Alberta, Canada, Burleson is a former football commentator and ex-NFL wide receiver.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Burleson's net worth reportedly stands at $18million.

Per the outlet, Burleson reportedly earned an annual $2.7million salary for his work as a football commentator for CBS Sports and the NFL Network.

Burleson's salary reportedly earns about $2million on This Morning.

The 40-year-old played college football for the Nevada Wolf Pack and was drafted into the NFL by the Minnesota Vikings in the third round of the 2003 Draft.

Burleson spent 11 years in the league, which included stints with the Seattle Seahawks, Cleveland Browns, and Detroit Lions.

Burleson owns the Seahawk team record for the most punt return yards in a single season (2007) and career punt return yards.

After his retirement, Burleson went into sports commentating, where he works with CBS as a studio analyst on The NFL Today.

He was also a co-host on Good Morning Football on NFL Network before stepping down in August 2021 and joining CBS Mornings the following month.

In March 2023, Nate is set to co-host the Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards alongside Charli D'Amelio.

What were the changes to CBS This Morning?

The American morning show is still recognized as the show viewers know and love.

"All CBS News morning broadcasts will now be part of the same family, with a focus on original reporting and exquisite storytelling, connected by the sound of the iconic trumpet music and an ethos of optimism that carries all the way through to the sunshine logo itself," Khemlani said in a statement last year.

"We're adding a little bit of 'CBS Sunday Morning' – every morning – on 'CBS Mornings.'"
