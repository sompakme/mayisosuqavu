Israel Adesanya, who was born on July 22, 1989, is a professional athlete from New Zealand. He has won several championships in boxing, kickboxing, and mixed martial arts (MMA). Adesanya currently competes in the UFC's Middleweight division and is the former UFC Middleweight champion. He currently holds a professional MMA record of 24 wins and 3 losses (24-3-0).

What is Israel Adesanya’s Net Worth in 2023?

As of August 2023, Israel Adesanya has an estimated net worth of $4 Million. He has earned substantial amounts of money from his fights in the UFC, where he has become a well-known fighter and a former champion in his division. Adesanya has also won numerous championships in kickboxing and MMA, which has further contributed to his wealth. Along with his fighting career, Adesanya has also received sponsorships and endorsements from brands like Puma and Engage.

Israel Adesanya’s Salary

According to reports, Israel Adesanya consistently earned more than $1 Million in his last five fights.

In his second fight against Alex Pereira at UFC 287, Adesanya earned a massive paycheck of $3.18 Million, which included $1 Million in base salary, $2 Million in pay-per-view sales, $100,000 in win bonus, $50,000 in a UFC bonus, and $32,000 in sponsorship deals.

Israel Adesanya’s Endorsements

Israel Adesanya's success as a professional athlete has allowed him to secure numerous endorsement deals. One of the most notable deals was with Puma, making him the first-ever MMA fighter to land a deal with the company. In September 2020, Adesanya signed a multi-year deal with Puma and was announced as the face of their Oceania division. He was also set to be a part of their global plans.

Along with Puma, Adesanya has also secured an endorsement deal with fight gear supplier Engage. These endorsement deals, coupled with his successful fighting career, have helped Adesanya increase his net worth and establish himself as a prominent figure in the world of combat sports.

Adesanya is also endorsed by PRIME, a hydration company.

Israel Adesanya’s Charity & Foundation Work

Israel Adesanya has previously stated in interviews that he frequently donates to multiple charities.
