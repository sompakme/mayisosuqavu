Dominik Mysterios Response to CM Punk Feud Speculation After Recent Tease

Dominik Mysterio is undoubtedly one of the biggest heels in WWE right now, who has truly come into his own as part of The Judgment Day. CM Punk recently teased a feud with him on social media and now Mysterio decided to address speculation that he will face Punk soon.

CM Punk was fired from AEW back in September following his altercation with Jack Perry at AEW All In. This eventually led to CM Punk’s return at the Survivor Series Premium Live Event – shocking the whole world in the process.

Punk already a cut a promo on RAW this past week but he wasn’t done there, as The Second City Saint teased feud with Dominik Mysterio on social media, as Punk shared a screenshot from the birthday celebration for Mysterio’s sister, Aalyah.

The NXT Champion finally responded to CM Punk’s tease, as he uploaded a party-themed variation of the image on his own Instagram Stories, where he put clown emojis on the faces of his family members. He also included a middle finger at the bottom, signifying his intention of a feud with Punk.

WWE is also internally pushing for a CM Punk dream match at WrestleMania 40. CM Punk’s first major feud will be with familiar foe. It remains to be seen how he will be booked in the coming months.
