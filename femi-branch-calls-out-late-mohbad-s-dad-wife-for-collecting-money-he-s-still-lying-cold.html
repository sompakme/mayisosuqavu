Femi Branch Calls Out Late Mohbads Dad &amp; Wife for Collecting Money: Hes Still Lying Cold

  • Nigerian actor Femi Branch drew the attention of Nigerians to a controversial situation going on around Mohbad's family
  • The Nollywood star mentioned that it was all shades of wrong for the late singer's wife and father to be collecting money from mourners
  • Speaking further, the actor warned against using Mohbad's death to catch cruise, saying justice was yet to be served

Nigerian actor Femi Branch has directed the attention of mourners towards the wrongdoings of the wife and father of the late singer Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba, known professionally as Mohbad.

In a heated session on social media video, the movie star mentioned that it was distasteful for the departed singer's wife and father to accept donations from fans while his boldly is still "lying cold on the mortuary.

The actor mentioned that Imole's wife and dad should be much more concerned about getting justice for the deceased artist.

He said the police conference ended up confusing Nigerians more. Femi said they did not tell the public what they did not already know. According to him, justice has not been served yet until the culprits have paid for the crime.

In his words:

"As a woman, they k!lled your husband, and as a father, they k!lled your child and what both of you could do was to drop account number for people to donate money. You don't even know the cause of the poor boy's death. "Were you suffering before he d!ed? Mohbad is lying cold in the mortuary and his father is barbing hair...no one is bothered about him getting justice. Nigerians stop sending money to these people. Everyone is seeing his spirit wandering and crying for justice yet his wife and father are busy collecting money. We are not even near getting justice for him. What kind of nonsense is this?"
