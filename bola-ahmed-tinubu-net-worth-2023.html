Bola Ahmed Tinubu Net Worth 2023

Bola Ahmed Tinubu is a famous Nigerian politician and former Governor of Lagos State who is bidding to become president of Nigeria.

Tinubu started his political career as a member of the Social Democratic Party (SDP) in the 1990s and later co-founded the Alliance for Democracy (AD) in 1998.

He was elected as Governor of Lagos State in 1999, serving two terms until 2007.

After his tenure as Governor, Tinubu became a key political figure in Nigeria, serving as a national leader of the Action Congress of Nigeria (ACN), which later merged with other political parties to form the All Progressives Congress (APC) in 2013.

The APC went on to win the Nigerian presidential election in 2015, with Muhammadu Buhari as its candidate.

Tinubu has been widely credited with playing a key role in the formation and success of the APC, and he remains an influential figure in Nigerian politics.

He has also been the subject of various controversies and allegations of corruption but has not been convicted of any crimes.

Bola Ahmed Tinubu was born on March 29, 1952, in Lagos, Nigeria.
