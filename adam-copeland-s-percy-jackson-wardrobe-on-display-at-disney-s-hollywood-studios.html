Adam Copelands Percy Jackson Wardrobe on Display at Disneys Hollywood Studios

WWE Hall of Famer Adam Copeland is a man of many talents, as he has a lot of acting experience apart from his pro wrestling career. This includes his latest venture in the ‘Percy Jackson’ show and now it appears his wardrobe from the series is on display at Disney’s Hollywood Studios.

Adam Copeland portrayed the role of Ares in the television adaptation of Rick Riordan’s acclaimed series, “Percy Jackson & The Olympians.” Naturally, fans were very excited for the series.

Copeland has already appeared in the show and his episode turned out to be one of the most well-received in the show so far, which isn’t really difficult to see why considering how well he portrayed his role as the God of War.

Adam Copeland took to Twitter and commented on a few photos which revealed that his wardrobe as Ares is on display at Walt Disney Presents at Disney’s Hollywood Studios. Copeland made it clear that he was happy about this.

”Gotta say this wasn’t on my bingo card for 2024, but I’ll take it. Life is crazy.”

It is also to be noted that the Rated R Superstar will continue his acting career despite his AEW run. We will have to wait and see whether Adam Copeland will take on more acting projects soon.
