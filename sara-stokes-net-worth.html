About

Singer and actress who first rose to fame competing on the second series of Making the Band in 2002 as a member of Da Band. She has since become a regular face for the BET reality series The House of Divas.

Before Fame

She studied at the Michigan College of Beauty before beginning her musical and acting career. Between appearing on Making the Band and The House of Divas, she also worked as a makeup artist for It Cosmetics.

Trivia

She made her acting debut in a small role in the 2002 film 8 Mile starring Eminem.

Family Life

She was jailed in 2014 for domestic violence charges against her husband Tony. He filed for divorce two days after her incarceration. Together they had three children named Elijah, Eva, and Gabby.

Associated With

A central face on The House of Divas, she shares the screen with host and model Claudia Jordan.
