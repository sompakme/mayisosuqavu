Melissa Etheridge Marries Linda Wallem!

Melissa Etheridge officially wants Linda Wallem to come to her window… forever.

The singer married the television producer exchanged vows yesterday (two days after each turned 53) at the San Ysidro Ranch in Montecito, CA.

“They were so happy,” a guest tells People. “It was really beautiful [and] such a magical moment.”

Etheridge actually performed a song she had written about her now-wife for the very first time as part of the celebration.

 

All four of Etheridge’s kids participated in the wedding, while famous guests included Jane Lynch, Chelsea Handler, Rosie O’Donnell, Whitney Cummings and Peter Facinelli.

“The ceremony was such a beautiful start to the night,” says the guest. “The setting was perfect. It was something Melissa and Linda wanted early on. It was as perfect as they envisioned.”

Etheridge had previously been with Tammy Lynn Michaels for very many years and recently settled an ugly custody battle with her ex.
