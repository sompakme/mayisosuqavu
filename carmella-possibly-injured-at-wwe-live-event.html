Carmella Possibly Injured At WWE Live Event

Carmella worked hard to make a name for herself in Triple H’s version of NXT. The second-generation wrestler made the most out of the opportunity when she got called to the main roster. Now there is unfortunate news to report.

Carmella recently competed at a WWE live event in North Charleston. The Most Beautiful Woman in the WWE squared off against Bianca Belair and Asuka in a triple threat match for the Raw Women’s Championship.

Fans in attendance reported, and it quickly spread like wildfire on Twitter, after the event is that Carmella was injured during the match. The nature of the injury has not been officially disclosed yet. Carmella’s husband Corey Graves also tweeted about her injury, but ended up deleting it.

In a now-deleted tweet, Corey Graves vented a bit about the matter. “LOVE learning that my f*cking wife got injured at a live event via Twitter,” the WWE commentator tweeted out before swiftly deleting it. You can check out a screen grab of that tweet below.

Carmella last televised challenge for the Raw Women’s Championship took place on July 18th, 2022. Mella failed to win the title from Bianca Belair, but revealed she was still proud of herself. We will continue monitoring this story here at Ringside News.
