Nic Kerdiles Death: Ex-NHL player Nic Kerdiles Cause of Death?

I’m truly sorry to hear about the tragic passing of former Anaheim Ducks player Nic Kerdiles in a motorcycle crash in North Nashville. Kerdiles, a talented hockey player known for his time with the Ducks and his engagement to reality TV star Savannah Chrisley, left a significant impact on the world of hockey and beyond. In this article, we’ll delve into the details surrounding the accident, Nic Kerdiles’ hockey career, and the heartfelt responses from his loved ones and the hockey community.

Nic Kerdiles: A Promising Hockey Career

Nicolas Kerdiles was an emerging young hockey player when selected by the Anaheim Ducks in the second round of the 2012 NHL Draft as part of their 2012 entry class, marking his professional debut. Kerdiles from Irvine California made history when he became their inaugural Orange County member to join their roster in 2017.

His journey to the NHL was marked by dedication and talent honed at the University of Wisconsin-Madison where he played for the Wisconsin Badgers. Kerdiles’ exceptional skills helped lead them to Western Collegiate Hockey Association and Big Ten Tourney championships; further cementing his legacy within college hockey.

Tragic Accident Claims Nic Kerdiles’ Life

The hockey world was shocked and saddened to learn of Nic Kerdiles’ untimely death on Saturday, the result of a motorcycle crash in North Nashville. The tragic incident occurred around 3:30 a.m. at the intersection of 14th Avenue North and Wheless Street when Kerdiles, riding his Indian Motorcycle, collided with a BMW SUV after running a stop sign.

Despite the severity of the collision, authorities reported that there were no signs of impairment from either driver involved in the accident. Kerdiles was promptly transported to Vanderbilt University Medical Center, where, sadly, he succumbed to his injuries.

The Nashville Metro Police stated that no charges are anticipated in this case, suggesting that the accident was a tragic, unforeseen event. The loss of Nic Kerdiles deeply affected not only his family and friends but also the entire hockey community, which mourned the passing of this talented athlete and wonderful individual.

Grief and Condolences Pour In

Nic Kerdiles’ impact extended far beyond the hockey rink, as evidenced by his engagement to Savannah Chrisley, a well-known reality TV personality. The couple appeared together on the reality TV shows “Chrisley Knows Best” and “Growing up Chrisley” from 2018 to 2019, sharing moments of their life together with the world.

Their relationship, unfortunately, came to an end in 2020, but the bond they shared remained strong. In response to Kerdiles’ tragic passing, Savannah Chrisley took to her Instagram Stories to express her profound grief. She shared a touching photo of the couple, lamenting the loss of her former fiancé and hoping for a reunion in the afterlife.

“Heaven gained the most beautiful angel today,” one of her posts read. “We loved hard… and I can’t wait to ride bikes along the beaches of heaven with you one day.” Her heartfelt words resonated with many who were moved by their love story.

Hockey World Mourns a Loss

The hockey community also came together to pay their respects to Nic Kerdiles, a player who had left an indelible mark on the sport. The Anaheim Ducks, his former team, issued a statement on social media, expressing their heartbreak over the tragic accident. They highlighted Kerdiles’ unique distinction as the first Orange County player to join the Ducks, acknowledging his contributions during his time with the team from 2016 to 2018.

USA Hockey, the governing body of ice hockey in the United States, joined in mourning Kerdiles’ passing. They noted his significant role in helping Team USA secure two gold medals at the International Ice Hockey U18 World Championship. Nic Kerdiles’ achievements on the international stage showcased his talent and dedication to the sport, making him a respected figure in the hockey world.

Remembering Nic Kerdiles: A Badger at Heart

Nic Kerdiles first made an impactful statement at the University of Wisconsin where he made an impactful mark as one of their Badger hockey team members. A member of both Western Collegiate Hockey Association and Big Ten Tourney tournaments, his presence brought great spirit and helped contribute to success during college years.

As hockey mourns Nic Kerdiles’ untimely passing serves as a stark reminder of life’s fragility, it also underscores its significance and underscores how profound an impact he left upon those he knew – an impactful mark etched onto their hearts that will endure for years.
