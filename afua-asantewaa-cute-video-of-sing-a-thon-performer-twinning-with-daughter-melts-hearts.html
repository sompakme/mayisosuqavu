Afua Asantewaa: Cute Video Of Sing-A-Thon Performer Twinning with Daughter Melts Hearts

  • A rare video has surfaced of Afua Asantewaa Aduonum and her daughter dancing to Anabella by Nigerian singer Khaid
  • The mother-daughter combo had a striking likeness that had peeps asking if the child was Afua Asantewaa's sister
  • Netizens who commented on her TikTok clip expressed their admiration for the pair

A rare video of Afua Asantewaa Aduonum twinning with her daughter as they grooved to the song Anabella by Nigerian singer Sulaimon Shekoni Solomon, known professionally as Khaid, has warmed hearts.

The clip has gained attention amid her attempt to become the Guinness World Record holder of the longest singing marathon by an individual. Sunil Waghmare of India holds the record after singing for 105 hours from March 3 to March 7, 2012.

Afua Asantewaa gave fans a rare glimpse of her happy times and bonding moments with her daughter. The video of the mother-daughter duo was taken in a setting with red curtains. The pair wore casual outfits for the heartwarming moments.

Ghanaian singer sets sights on breaking the longest singing streak

Earlier, Legit.ng reported that Afua Asantewaa, a renowned Ghanaian singer, launched her attempt at the five-day singathon challenge in a bid to break the Guinness World Record for the longest singing streak.

To kick off her performance, the talented vocalist chose African-made dresses and began with renditions of popular songs by leading female African artists.

Her initiative garnered admiration from netizens who praised her determination, wishing her the necessary stamina to successfully accomplish the challenge.
