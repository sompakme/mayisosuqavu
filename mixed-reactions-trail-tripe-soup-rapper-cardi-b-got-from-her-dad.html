Tripe is not exactly everyone's cup of tea but Cardi B loves it. Taking to social media, the WAP hitmaker decided to show off the tripe that she was about to dig into.

Cardi was super excited to have a taste of one of her favourite foods.

"Who likes this besides me? I got it from my dad," said Cardi B.

Fans had mixed reactions to Cardi's food. Tripe is not really the first thing that people order when going to a restaurant, especially not Americans.
