What Happened To Drew Scott?

Andrew Alfred Scott, better known as Drew Scott, is a Canadian actor, realtor, and entrepreneur best known for co-hosting the popular TV series “Property Brothers” alongside his twin brother Jonathan Scott. The show, which has been a staple on HGTV since 2011, features the brothers as they help clients purchase and renovate “fixer-upper” homes. Despite his high-profile status on television, fans have often wondered about Drew’s personal life, career developments, and other ventures outside of the “Property Brothers” series. This article delves into the life and career of Drew Scott, providing a comprehensive overview of what happened to him.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

  • Born on April 28, 1978, in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.
  • Developed an interest in acting and performing at a young age.
  • Began his career as an actor with appearances in small roles on television shows.
  • Transitioned into real estate and, alongside his brother Jonathan, started investing in properties.

Breakthrough with “Property Brothers”

  • The show premiered in 2011 and quickly gained popularity.
  • Drew serves as the real estate expert, while Jonathan is the contractor.
  • Their dynamic and expertise have made the show a success, leading to several spin-offs.

Personal Life

  • Married his long-time girlfriend Linda Phan in May 2018.
  • Active on social media, sharing aspects of his personal life with fans.
  • Known for his philanthropic efforts and involvement in various charities.

Expansion of the Scott Brothers Brand

  • Launched Scott Brothers Entertainment, a production company.
  • Expanded into lifestyle branding with furniture and home decor lines.
  • Authored books with his brother, including “Dream Home” and “It Takes Two: Our Story”.

Health and Wellness

Public Appearances and Media Involvement

Philanthropy and Community Work

Recent Projects and Ventures

Challenges and Controversies

Impact on Real Estate and Television

Public Perception and Fan Base

Future Endeavors

FAQ Section

What is Drew Scott’s background in real estate?

Drew Scott has a background as a real estate agent and began investing in properties at a young age with his brother Jonathan. His expertise in real estate has been a cornerstone of the “Property Brothers” show, where he helps clients find and purchase homes to renovate.

Has Drew Scott won any awards for his work?

While Drew Scott has not won any major individual awards, “Property Brothers” has received nominations for various awards, including the Emmy Awards. The show’s success is a testament to the hard work and dedication of both Drew and Jonathan.

What are some of Drew Scott’s hobbies?

Drew Scott enjoys activities such as basketball, horseback riding, and outdoor adventures. He also has a passion for acting and performing, which was his initial career path before entering the world of real estate and television.

Conclusion

Drew Scott has become a household name through his work on “Property Brothers” and his various business and philanthropic endeavors. Alongside his brother Jonathan, Drew has made a significant impact on the real estate and entertainment industries. His personal life, marked by his marriage to Linda Phan and his active social media presence, has endeared him to fans around the world. Despite facing industry challenges and the occasional controversy, Drew has maintained a positive public image and continues to pursue new projects and ventures. With his dedication to philanthropy and community work, Drew Scott is not just a television personality but a figure who uses his influence for the greater good. As he looks to the future, fans can expect to see more of Drew’s creativity and passion in all his endeavors.
