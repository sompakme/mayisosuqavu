Bling Empire: What is Andrew Grays net worth? Wed 20 January 2021 21:31 Updated Tue 17 Ja

If you were a fan of Selling Sunset and Love Island, then Netflix’s brand new eight part reality television show, Bling Empire, is perfect for you.

The show is reminiscent of a real- life Crazy Rich Asians (2018) and is based on the lives of a group of extremely wealthy Asian and Asian-American residents of Los Angeles.

One of the cast members, Andrew Gray, may be recognised for his work as an actor, but this time he is appearing on our screens as the partner of Kelly Mi Li, an entrepreneur.

Who is Andrew Gray from Bling Empire?

Andrew Gray is the partner of Kelly Mi Li, an independent entrepreneur who has worked in real estate, the food and drink sector, and tech investment.

Andrew himself has made a name for himself, playing the role of Troy Burrows, the Red Megaforce Ranger in Power Rangers Megaforce.

He is 33 years-old and is both Mexican and Native American.

Andrew describes himself as an actor, producer and entrepreneur.
