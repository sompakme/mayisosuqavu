The French Bikini Is the One Swimwear Trend That Works for Small and Large Chests

Cool French-girl style has developed a reputation for being that effortlessly chic, je ne sais quoi for which we are constantly on the hunt. There are the Breton stripes, the lace lingerie sets, the little black dresses — the list goes on. But, there's one summertime trend that is one of the most sought-after Parisian looks, and for good reason. That, of course, is the French bikini.

Popularized during the 1960s and '70s via icons like Jane Birkin and Twiggy (though not French herself), this over-the-shoulder tank style was revolutionary for its sporty-yet-sweet design. Not quite as revealing as a string bikini yet lighter than an underwire style, it's no surprise that it's a swimwear trend that has remained largely untouched — and is still just as popular — today.

Not only is the French bikini one of the chicest swimwear silhouettes, as it turns out, it's one of the few that is actually equally flattering, comfortable, and easy-to-wear for both large and small chests alike. Which, to be honest, is quite a feat.

When it comes to swimwear trends, there seem to be very few designs that support small and large chests in the same effortless manner. Sure, anyone can wear a sporty one-piece, but it might not offer enough support for a larger cup size. We can all clasp ourselves into an underwire top and some retro high-waist bottoms, but that option may prove quite uncomfortable for those less endowed. The French bikini, however, is differernt.

For starters, the French bikini's shape offers enough coverage for large cup sizes without feeling restrictive or overwhelming. And, on the flip side, it still boasts a dipping neckline that gives smaller chests a boost as well. The real magic, though, comes by way of the shoulder straps, which offer a more comfortable and more supportive construction than a halter, thus eliminating the desperate need for a structured underwire in most cases.

Though it's the swimwear style that I personally have worn exclusively for years, I invite you to try it out or yourself, just in time for warmer weather. Shop some of my favorites below!
