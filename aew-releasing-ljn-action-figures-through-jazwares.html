AEW Releasing LJN Action Figures Through Jazwares

AEW is all about pro wrestling history and their action figures will follow that lead. LJN figures are loved throughout the collectors market and tons of fans still scout out for the missing piece to their collection. The company is set to release their own LJN design with Jazwares.

This first line of LJN figures will include that classic look for the first time in 30 years. They only gave out the design for Cody’s figure in the reveal so we’ll have to see what they do with the rest of the line.

Jeremy Padawer also revealed new figures for Cody Rhodes, Kenny Omega, Dustin Rhodes, Darby Allin, and Miro.

Britt Baker already threatened to retire if she didn’t get an AEW action figure soon. She could retire if she decided to fall back on her other great career as a legit dentist. She’s getting a figure as well.
