What Happened To Deandre Hopkins Mom?

The story of DeAndre Hopkins and his mother, Sabrina Greenlee, is one of tragedy, resilience, and profound love. Greenlee’s life took a dramatic and violent turn that not only affected her but also played a significant role in shaping the character and career of her son, DeAndre Hopkins, a prominent NFL wide receiver. This article delves into the harrowing events that befell Sabrina Greenlee and how she emerged as a source of inspiration for many.

The Early Life of Sabrina Greenlee

Sabrina Greenlee was born in South Carolina and is the mother of four children, including DeAndre Hopkins. She worked multiple jobs to support her family and instilled a strong work ethic in her children. Greenlee faced numerous challenges throughout her life, including domestic violence and the difficulties of being a single mother.

The Attack on Sabrina Greenlee

In 2002, Sabrina Greenlee’s life changed forever. She was the victim of an acid attack that left her blind and with severe burns over her body. The incident occurred when Greenlee was lured to a parking lot by a woman who then threw a mixture of lye and bleach in her face. The attack was motivated by jealousy over a man they were both involved with.

The Aftermath of the Attack

Following the attack, Greenlee spent weeks in a medically induced coma. She underwent numerous surgeries and faced a long, painful recovery process. Despite the physical and emotional trauma, Greenlee’s determination to survive and be there for her children never wavered.

Legal Proceedings

The woman responsible for the attack was apprehended and faced legal consequences for her actions. The severity of the crime brought attention to issues of domestic violence and the impact of such attacks on victims and their families.

DeAndre Hopkins’ Response to His Mother’s Plight

DeAndre Hopkins was only 10 years old when his mother was attacked. The incident had a profound impact on him, shaping his perspective on life and strengthening his resolve to succeed. Hopkins has often spoken about the motivation he draws from his mother’s resilience and her ability to overcome adversity.

Supporting His Mother

Sabrina Greenlee’s Recovery and Advocacy
