The Hills star Whitney Port reveals she's pregnant with her first child

    Whitney Port has announced she is pregnant with her first child. The star took to her Instagram page on Thursday to announce the happy news, revealing that she and her husband Tim Rosenman are "so excited" with the impending arrival. In the caption, she wrote: "Oh hey! Just standing by the window in my underwear, with a BABY in my belly!!! DM me if you know what I'm supposed to do with this thing for however many years I'm supposed to be in charge... We are sooooo excited!!!!"

    This will be Whitney and her producer partner's first child. The couple tied the knot in Palm Springs in November 2015 after meeting on the set spin-off, . The reality TV star, who now has her own fashion label, also opened up about her pregnancy in her blog. "When two people love each other so much and the love becomes too great to fit inside both people, it spills over into a THIRD person," she wrote. "This is how I am going to explain where babies come from to the little baby that is now growing inside my belly because Timmy and I are PREGNANT!"
